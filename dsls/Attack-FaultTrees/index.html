Attack-Fault Trees

A framework to capture the temporal and causal security and safety interactions in reliability block diagrams (RBD) through Attack-fault trees (AFTs). AFTs are a formalism for the combined safety-security analysis that integrates both attack trees and fault trees. https://doi.org/10.1109/HASE.2017.12 https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-62840-6_14
